## ⏰ Schedule

### 🔧 How to Create a Schedule

1. Open your pipeline.
2. Click **Schedule** at the top.
3. Select **+ New**.
4. Fill in:
   - **Start date and time**
   - **Frequency** (Daily, Hourly, etc.)
   - **Time zone**
5. Click **Create**.

💡 Now your pipeline will run on that schedule!
